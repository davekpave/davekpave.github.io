Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 24, 2025

Rizzard Games ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information in our mobile game ("the App"), available on the Google Play Store.

1. Information We Collect

  • Leaderboard Data: If you choose to submit your score to the online leaderboard, we collect your chosen player name and selected country. This information is stored in our online database (Firebase/Firestore) and is visible to other players.
  • Ads: The App displays rewarded ads using Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device information and usage data as described in Google’s Privacy Policy.
  • Local Game Data: Your game progress (such as completed levels and local leaderboard) is stored locally on your device. We do not access or transmit this data to our servers.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display your name and country on the online leaderboard.
  • To provide rewarded ads and track ad rewards.
  • To save your game progress and scores locally.

3. Children’s Privacy

We take privacy seriously, especially for children. The App is designed to be safe for all ages and does not contain:

  • In-app purchases
  • External links or social sharing

If a parent or guardian believes that we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately (see contact below).

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Used for rewarded ads. AdMob may collect device and usage data.
  • Firebase/Firestore: Used to store and display leaderboard data (player name and country).

Please review the privacy policies of these providers for more information.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes to our practices. The latest version will always be available within the App or on our website.
